Founder

Syed Ejaz Gillani

Syed Ejaz Digital Library is a knowledge-driven platform dedicated to promoting literature, education, and community awareness. This platform reflects the lifelong contributions and literary journey of a distinguished writer, journalist, and educator who has been actively serving in the field of media, literature, and social development since the 1980s.

In the early years of his career, he founded the Gulistan Literary Society, Chunian (District Kasur), aiming to promote literary activities and nurture young talent. In 1983, he launched the children’s magazine Gulistan as Chief Editor, providing meaningful and educational content for young readers.

From 1987 to 2000, he served as the Editor of the monthly Sahil Rang Kasur, contributing significantly to regional journalism and literary growth. He has also remained associated with the well-known social organization Awami Kulti Anjuman (Kasur) as Director of Information and Publications, playing a key role in public awareness initiatives.

He holds a Master’s degree (MA) in Punjabi Language and Literature from Punjab University, Lahore, which further strengthened his literary and academic foundation.

Throughout his career, he has worked with leading Pakistani newspapers and magazines, including Daily Imroz, Mashriq, and Jang (Lahore) as an article writer. He also contributed to the Family Magazine of Nawa-e-Waqt as a sub-editor and feature writer.

In addition, he served as a correspondent for renowned English newspapers such as The News (Lahore) and The Frontier Post (Lahore), highlighting his versatility in both Urdu and English journalism.

Our Story

Founded on the principle that knowledge is a universal heritage, the Syed Ejaz Digital Library began as a private collection of rare manuscripts. Over the decades, we recognized the fragility of physical archives and the urgent need for a resilient, accessible alternative.
